# Overview

To authenticate with Anvil, you will need:
1. **API Key** - A key that grants Nango permission to interact with Anvil's APIs resources and services.

This guide will walk you through generating your API Key within Anvil.

### Prerequisites:
- You must have a registered account with Anvil.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your API Key
1. Log in to your [Anvil dashboard](https://app.useanvil.com/login).
2. In the lower left hand corner, click on the building icon above your avatar or initials.
3. Click on the gear icon to go to the Organization Settings.
4. Click on **API** in the left hand tray.
5. Generate a new key or copy the existing **Production** API Key

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Anvil.
2. Enter your **API Key** in the designated field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to Anvil.
